Step 1 · Output plan

Choose whether you need one paystub or a guided multi-stub sequence.

Step 2 · Company and employee

Enter who is paying and who is being paid.

Step 3 · Pay schedule

Set the pay frequency, the pay period, and whether the selected date is the first or latest stub in the sequence.

Step 4 · Earnings

Choose salary or hourly pay for the regular line, then add any extra earnings such as overtime, bonus, or holiday pay.

Step 5 · Taxes

Federal, FICA, and state taxes are calculated automatically after the pay setup above is complete.

Step 6 · Deductions

Leave this section empty unless you need a deduction line. Only deductions you add here will appear on the stub.

Step 7 · Adjustments

Add reimbursements, employer additions, or corrections that should appear separately from deductions.

Step 8 · Benefits and balances

Leave this section empty unless you need to show an extra benefit or balance line such as PTO, sick time, or an employer-paid item.
